Platform Garanti binasýnda kablolar / Cables on Platform Garanti building




Ilk kez Platform Garanti binasýnýn 4 katýnda 4 ayrý ses sistemiyle çaldýðýmýz 4/4' ün stüdyo kaydý.
Studio recording of '4/4' which we premiered wýth 4 seperate PAs installed in 4 floors of Platform Garanti building.

31 January 2013 BPM: 135

Cem: Gitar/Guitar Cevdet: Davul/Drums Kerem: Bas/Bass Gökhan: Gitar/Guitar

Davul ve Cem'in gitar Maslak 1024'te, Bas Kerem'in evinde ve Gökhan'ýn gitar Afacan sokakta evde Ocak 2013'de kaydedildi. Edit, miks ve mastering Maslak 1024'te hep beraber yapýldý.
Kapak resmi Platform Garanti için Cevdet'in çizdiði el ilaný.
Murat 1024 Gülbay ve daimi sesçimiz Gökhan Deneç'e sonsuz teþekkürler.

Drums and Cem's guitar were recorded at Maslak 1024, bass was recorded at Kerem's house and Gökhan's guitar was recorded at home in Afacan street in January 2013. Editing, mixing and mastering was done all together at Maslak 1024.
Cover image is Platform Garanti handout prepared by Cevdet.
Many thanks to Murat 1024 Gülbay and our permanent tonmaister Gökhan Deneç.
